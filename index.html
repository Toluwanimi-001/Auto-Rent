Privacy Policy

AutoRent Nigeria Ltd collects personal information strictly for the purpose of processing your reservation and communicating with you regarding your rental.

Data we collect: Name, phone number, email address, driver's licence details, and trip preferences.

How we use it: To process bookings, provide support, and improve our service. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Security: All data is stored on encrypted servers compliant with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019.

Your rights: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting privacy@autorentng.com.

Terms of Service

Eligibility: Drivers must hold a valid Nigerian or international driver's licence and be at least 21 years of age. A valid NIN or national ID is required at pickup.

Rental period: All rentals are calculated on a 24-hour basis. Late returns beyond 2 hours incur an additional day's charge.

Fuel policy: Vehicles are provided with a full tank and must be returned full. Fuel deficits are charged at market rate plus ₦2,500 service fee.

Liability: AutoRent is not liable for personal property left in vehicles. Drivers are responsible for traffic fines incurred during their rental period.

Insurance: Comprehensive third-party insurance is included. Full coverage upgrades are available at booking.

Cancellation Policy

48+ hours before pickup: Full refund with no cancellation fee.

24–48 hours before pickup: 50% refund of total booking value.

Under 24 hours before pickup: No refund. A credit note valid for 90 days may be issued at management's discretion.

No-show: Treated as a cancellation under 24 hours. The vehicle will be released after 2 hours of the agreed pickup time.

To cancel, contact us via WhatsApp on +234 812 345 6789 or email reservations@autorentng.com.
